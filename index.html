IMPORTANCE OF PHYSICAL EDUCATION

Studies have demonstrated that physical activity, including team sports, biking, swimming at the beach, and playground games, has positive effects on the brain and academic achievement. Children learn how to be motivated and productive, fit and healthy for life, as well as how to think creatively through physical education. It has been demonstrated that participation in sports, physical education, and physical activity increases students sense of connection to their school and its objectives.
